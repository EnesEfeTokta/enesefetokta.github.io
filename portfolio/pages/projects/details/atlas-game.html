Atlas

A multiplayer sci-fi strategy game where two players compete to dominate a planet through strategic base building and tactical warfare

Unity 3D C# Photon Fusion Multiplayer Low Poly Strategy
View Repository

Game Overview

Atlas is an online multiplayer strategy game built with Photon Fusion where two players land on a planet and compete for dominance. Players construct towers, produce military units, and engage in strategic combat to destroy their opponent's main base.

The game features a sci-fi theme with low poly aesthetics, creating an immersive yet performance-friendly multiplayer experience. With real-time networking and strategic depth, Atlas delivers intense competitive gameplay.

Four Unique Factions

Each faction has distinctive characteristics, units, and playstyles that offer different strategic approaches:

SOLGUARD

Defensive specialists with enhanced energy towers and superior base fortifications.

SILASYS

Technology-focused faction with advanced air units and precision weaponry.

FLORIA

Nature-aligned faction with unique regenerative abilities and organic unit production.

PYROCLAST

Aggressive faction specializing in heavy ground vehicles and explosive firepower.

Strategic Buildings & Military Units

Buildings

  • Energy Towers

    Generate resources needed for unit production and base operations.

  • Hangars

    Production facilities for both air and ground military units.

  • Main Base

    The core structure - destroy the enemy's main base to win the match.

Military Units

  • Air Units

    Fast-moving aircraft for quick strikes and reconnaissance missions.

  • Ground Units

    Heavy armored vehicles with powerful weapons for direct assaults.

Technical Features

  • Photon Fusion Networking

    Server-authoritative multiplayer with smooth prediction and lag compensation.

  • Low Poly Optimization

    Stylized graphics that maintain high performance in multiplayer scenarios.

  • Strategic Depth

    Resource management, base building, and tactical unit control systems.

  • Real-time Synchronization

    Seamless state synchronization for buildings, units, and combat actions.

Core Gameplay Loop

  1. 1
    Land on Planet

    Both players arrive on an unexplored planet and choose their faction.

  2. 2
    Build Infrastructure

    Construct energy towers to generate resources for expansion.

  3. 3
    Produce Military Units

    Build hangars and train air and ground forces for attack and defense.

  4. 4
    Strategic Warfare

    Execute coordinated attacks while defending your base from enemy assaults.

  5. 5
    Victory

    Destroy the enemy's main base to claim the planet and win the match!
