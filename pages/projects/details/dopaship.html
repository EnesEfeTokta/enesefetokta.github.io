The Purpose

Realistic Shopping, Zero Spending

Why do we shop? Is it for the product, or for the chemical rush we feel when click "order"? DopaShip is an interactive game designed to simulate the core retail therapy loops without touching your bank account.

By matching the visual, conversational, and delivery stages of high-end online retail apps, DopaShip recreates the exact psychological cycles of purchasing, waiting, and unboxing, helping you satisfy impulses in a fun and completely free space.

Game Features

Every Stage Simulated

Hilarious Fake Shopping Items

Browse a rich catalog of thousands of funny, simulated, or completely impossible products. Fill your shopping cart with futuristic vehicles, rare historical items, planetary real estate, and bizarre collectibles.

Detailed Shipping Tracker

The excitement doesn't stop at checkout. Track your fake package in real-time as it moves through processing, gets loaded onto delivery vessels, and approaches your imaginary home.

Realistic Payment Simulation

Experience the satisfaction of executing digital transactions. Use fake credit cards, mock coupons, and virtual wallets to finalize orders without any risk of debt.

Simulated Customer Chatbot

Encounter a mock issue with your order? Open a chat with our quirky customer support robot. Resolve problems, negotiate mock refunds, and converse through intuitive dialog trees.
