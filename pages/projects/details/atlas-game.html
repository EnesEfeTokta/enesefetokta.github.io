ATLAS

A high-stakes multiplayer strategy game set on Mars. Built with Unity and Photon Fusion for real-time competitive warfare.

Unity 3D C# Photon Fusion Multiplayer
PRIORITY ALPHA: MISSION_LOG

Conquer the Martian Crust

Atlas challenges two commanders to land, build, and destroy. Unlike traditional RTS games, Atlas focuses on a streamlined, high-speed economic loop and intense unit-to-unit synchronization.

The Four Factions

SOLGUARD

Defensive specialists. Features enhanced energy towers and superior base fortifications for long-term endurance.

Nature-aligned. Utilizes regenerative hull technologies and organic unit production cycles.
