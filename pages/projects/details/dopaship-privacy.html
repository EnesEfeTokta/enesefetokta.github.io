Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the DopaShip: Fake Shop mobile application developed by Enes Efe Tokta. DopaShip values user privacy and is committed to delivering a secure user experience.

1. Information We Collect

DopaShip: Fake Shop does not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses) from its users.

All shopping simulation mechanics and purchase flows in the game are completely fictional. The game does not connect to real-world payment networks and will never prompt you to enter credit card numbers or financial details.

2. Third-Party Services

The game may utilize third-party SDKs to facilitate core operating system systems or performance reporting. These services handle data in accordance with their own respective privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services

3. Cookies and Local Storage

DopaShip saves your game data (such as virtual balance, inventory, and achievements) locally on your device (using SharedPreferences / local storage). This data stays strictly on your physical device and is never transmitted to remote servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it immediately.
