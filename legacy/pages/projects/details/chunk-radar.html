Project Essence

Chunk-Radar is more than a study tool; it's a technical demonstration of Vibe-Coding effectiveness. It transforms the "Chunking Method"—the practice of learning word clusters—into a gamified, visual experience.

"Learn word patterns naturally, not just isolated definitions."
